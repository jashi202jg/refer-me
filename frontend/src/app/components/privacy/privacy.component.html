Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 6, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when creating an account, updating your profile, or nominating candidates. This includes your name, email address, phone number, company affiliation (for referrers), and any resume documents uploaded to the platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and improve the Service, including: connecting job seekers with referrers, sending notifications about referral status, and verifying professional affiliations.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Your resume and profile data are shared only with verified referrers or employers when you explicitly apply for a referral. We do not sell or trade your personal information to third-party advertisers or marketers.

4. Data Retention and Security

We employ standard security protocols to safeguard your personal data. We retain your information for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide you with the Service. You may request account deletion at any time.

5. Cookies and Trackers

We use session cookies to keep you logged in and preserve theme preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features of the platform may not function correctly as a result.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact our support team.
